ETRO shoes collection for men offers a surprising reinterpretation of classic models. Made of fine leather, they add a distinctive touch to every step. Choose a derby or monk strap combined with a tailored suit or elegant chino trousers for a truly unique look. Paisley sneakers are perfect to complete sporty looks. The fusion of sophisticated designs and comfort makes each pair of ETRO shoes an authentic discovery. Confront each day with confidence, walking in shoes that reflect your individuality. Create a sophisticated look and combine ETRO ankle and waterproof boots with a jacquard jacket, an oversize shirt and vintage effect jeans. Wear our clogs that match perfectly with a pair of culottes and a maxi Paisley cardigan. Shop now and discover the beauty of walking in shoes that go beyond a simple accessory, becoming an expression of your lifestyle.
